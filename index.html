About

A CORE MEMORY is all about capturing the moments that shape our lives.


I believe that certain memories stay with us forever — the quiet beginnings, the deep connections, and the fleeting moments that become part of who we are. These are the moments I love to preserve through natural and timeless photography.


Specializing in newborn, maternity, couple, and portrait photography in Berlin, I focus on creating authentic images filled with warmth, connection, and emotion.


As a mobile photographer, I come to your home or a location that feels meaningful to you, allowing you to relax and simply enjoy the moment while I turn it into a lasting memory.
