About

A CORE MEMORY is all about capturing the moments that shape our lives.


I believe that certain memories stay with us forever — the quiet beginnings, the deep connections, and the fleeting moments that become part of who we are.


Specializing in newborn, maternity, couple and portrait photography in Berlin, I create natural and timeless images filled with warmth, love and connection.
